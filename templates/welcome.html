A kanban board is one of the tools that can be used to implement kanban to manage work at a personal or organizational level.

Kanban boards visually depict work at various stages of a process using cards to represent work items and lists(columns) to represent each stage of the process. Cards are moved from left to right to show progress. Kanban can be used to organize many areas of an organization and can be designed accordingly. The simplest kanban board consists of three columns: "to-do", "doing" and "done".
Kanban Lists Each column on the board represents a different stage of your workflow. The cards go through the workflow until their full completion.
Kanban Cards This is the visual representation of tasks. Each card contains information about the task and its status, such as deadline, description, etc.
